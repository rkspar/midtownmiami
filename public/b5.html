How to look for a property (Explaining the MLS process)

In South America and in Europe you do not have a standard system that allows you to search for properties. The way it is done in other countries is the buyer asks a real estate agent, that real estate agent ask another real estate agent in another district and it is by word of mouth.

In the United States we have a system called MLS a Multiple Listing Service where up to 98% of all the properties for sale in the United States are in that system. In order to access the full information on that system you need to be a real estate agent registered with a real estate association.

When you ask for a real estate agent for a property she/ he will ask for the characteristics you are looking for. For example: 2 bedroom 2 bathroom, type of property, condominium, the price $500,000 to $550,000, the area Downtown Miami. The real estate agent will input that information into the MLS system and all the 2 bedroom and 2 bathroom condominiums between $500,000 to $550,000 in Downtown Miami will show in that search. Not only will the properties show in that search, but it will also show the name of the listing agent that is representing the seller. The agent can contact the sellerâ€™s agent and show the property to his client the potential buyer.

Also, the agent through this system able to see the history of the property meaning:

  • How many days that property has been on the market
  • What was the original price
  • How much the current owner bought that property for
  • How long ago was that property purchased
  • How much taxes the current owner pays on the property
  • Is the owner is current on the taxes
  • How much is the maintenance fee on that property
  • Other characteristics in the community like tennis courts, pools etc. in that building

This way we can standardize, automate and make it much easier, effective and simpler to search for properties in the United States. That is why you should work with a real estate agent whom is a realtor meaning he belongs a real estate association in that town. It can help not only searching for the property you are looking for, but also give you details about the property you selected to make an offer.
