Pet Policy

Tenants in Blue Condo Miami can have a maximum of 2 pets with the combined weight of 40lbs. There are no pet restrictions for owners! The building is perfect for the “crazy cat lady”!

Rental Policy

The minimum rental period is 3 months.

Cable and Internet

Basic cable is included in the building’s HOA fee. For the TV junkie, you can get an upgraded package from Hotwire for an additional charge. Internet is pre-wired, but not included in the HOA fee. There is an additional charge for internet.

Blue Condo Miami- If you have ever driven on the Julia Tuttle Causeway going towards Edgewater; you will notice the skyline starts with an awesome 36 story all glass tower shaped like a blue blade. Ladies and gentlemen this is Blue Condo! Blue Condo Miami was designed by world renowned architect, Arquitectonica, and features and incredible expansive view of the Biscayne Bay. The building is located at the center of Miami’s arteries which makes it easy to go anywhere. Within a 5 minute radius you can get to Miami Beach, Downtown, Midtown, Design District, Wynwood, I-95, and many of your daily conveniences. All units are designed with high end European style finishes. The building caters to those who have a modern taste and enjoy luxurious amenities. As and added benefit, Blue Condo residents have always enjoyed making all of their friends jealous!.
